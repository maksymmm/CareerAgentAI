# CareerAgent AI MVP

Version: 1.0

---

# Goal

Build the smallest possible version of CareerAgent AI that delivers real value to a user.

The MVP must be fully functional, stable, and extensible.

---

# User Story

A user creates a profile once.

The AI continuously searches for jobs matching the user's preferences.

The AI evaluates employers.

The AI prepares tailored resumes and cover letters.

The AI keeps track of all applications.

The AI assists with recruiter communication where appropriate.

The user only becomes actively involved when an interview or another important decision requires human participation.

---

# MVP Modules

## User Profile

Stores:

- personal information
- preferred job titles
- preferred locations
- salary expectations
- languages
- work permits
- resume
- cover letter templates

---

## Job Search

Supported sources:

- LinkedIn (within platform rules and available integrations)
- Indeed
- StepStone
- Arbeitnow
- Company career pages

---

## Employer Intelligence

Provides:

- Employer Score
- Salary estimation
- Public reviews
- Company size
- Hiring activity

---

## Application Manager

Tracks:

- Saved jobs
- Submitted applications
- Interviews
- Offers
- Rejections

---

## Dashboard

Displays:

- Today's activity
- Active applications
- AI recommendations
- Employer ratings
- Statistics

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP should save users significant time while improving the quality of their job search.

The architecture must support future expansion without major redesign.
