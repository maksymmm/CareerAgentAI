# CareerAgent AI Vision

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

---

# Vision

CareerAgent AI is an AI-powered career operating system.

Its purpose is to automate the entire job search process while allowing the user to focus only on meaningful career decisions.

CareerAgent AI acts as a trusted digital employee working continuously on behalf of the user.

The long-term vision is to eliminate repetitive job search tasks through responsible automation, intelligent decision support, and continuous learning.

---

# Mission

CareerAgent AI helps users spend their time preparing for opportunities instead of searching for them.

The system continuously:

- discovers relevant vacancies;
- analyzes job descriptions;
- matches opportunities to the user's profile;
- prepares tailored resumes and cover letters;
- manages application tracking;
- assists with recruiter communication where appropriate;
- prepares the user for interviews;
- learns from previous outcomes to improve future recommendations.

---

# Core Values

- Automation of repetitive work
- Transparency
- Privacy by design
- Security first
- Human oversight for important decisions
- Honest representation of the user's qualifications
- Continuous improvement through feedback

---

# Long-Term Goal

CareerAgent AI aims to become a complete AI Career Operating System that supports users throughout their professional journey, from discovering opportunities to preparing for interviews and planning long-term career growth.
